Koray Cosguner

Associate Professor of Marketing
John Rau KSB Faculty Fellow

Koray Cosguner stands at the intersection of marketing, economics, and psychology, leveraging rigorous quantitative methods to decode the complexities of consumer decision-making. His research transforms deep behavioral insights into high-impact firm strategies, optimizing pricing architectures and strategic levers across product, promotion, and placement.

An expert in structural modeling, reduced form estimation, and causal inference, he addresses critical business challenges ranging from dynamic pricing algorithms to developing advanced decision support systems for firm's email marketing decisions. His scholarship is consistently published in the field's premier outlets, including Marketing Science, Journal of Marketing Research, Management Science, and Journal of Retailing.
