Proprietary Analytics Tools

Our training includes hands-on access to a suite of interactive, web-based platforms covering pricing strategy, demand modeling, competitive analysis, and channel optimization:

Price Optimization & Elasticity

Calculate price and income elasticities from real data. Optimize prices under linear and constant-elasticity demand. Compare revenue vs. profit maximization.

Regression & Choice Modeling

Run linear, Poisson, and logistic regressions directly in the browser. Estimate multinomial and conditional logit models. Uncover hidden segments with latent class regression.

Conjoint & Experiment Design

Design fractional factorial experiments for conjoint analysis. Define product attributes and levels, generate efficient survey designs, and export profiles ready for analysis.

Competitive & Channel Strategy

Compute Bertrand-Nash equilibrium prices for competing brands. Optimize producer pricing across channel structures. Quantify double marginalization effects.

No coding required. Our tools let you perform sophisticated quantitative analysis without any programming or data science skills. Upload your data, click a few buttons, and get strategies, results, and presentation-ready figures instantly.

Meet the Principal

Dr. Koray Cosguner

Founder, CEO & Principal Consultant

Dr. Cosguner studies how customers make decisions and how firms can use that knowledge to improve performance. His research covers pricing and non-pricing instruments — including promotion, product, and placement — with a focus on turning data into actionable recommendations.

He teaches pricing and marketing analytics at Indiana University's Kelley School of Business, where he brings real business problems into the classroom and shows students how to solve them with data. The same frameworks he uses to train future marketing leaders, he brings to your business.

His work has been published in the field's top journals, featured in practitioner outlets, and implemented in field settings. He doesn't just build models — he builds data-driven decision support systems.
